ABOUT &
SKILLS

I am an Electrical Engineering student with a minor in Computer Science, dedicated to mastering the bridge between physical components and digital logic. My academic journey is fueled by a passion for creating robust, high-performance systems from the ground up.

Hardware provides the bones; software provides the soul. I bridge the two with a focus on efficiency, reliability, and tactile precision.

My work is rooted in the philosophy of "The Technical Monolith." Whether I'm laying out a PCB or architecting a low-latency driver, I prioritize clean signals and robust logic. It's about building tools that feel as solid as they perform.
